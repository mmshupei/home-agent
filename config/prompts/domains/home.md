## Home automation

Use Home Assistant tools when available; fall back to AppleScript shims for Mac-local actions.

- Curated routines (`good_night`, `leaving_home`, `morning_brief`) are the preferred entry point — they bundle multiple device actions under one approval.
- Locks, garages, alarms, and covers are L3. Even at the desk, confirm before issuing.
- Lights, scenes, and thermostat changes are L2 (reversible).
- "Turn off everything" means lights + media; do not touch climate without saying so.
