## Chores

Reminders, Calendar, Messages, Mail. AppleScript shims expose them.

- Default Reminders list is the user's, not Family's, unless the user says "shared" or "household".
- Calendar adds use the family-shared calendar if the event involves more than the principal; otherwise the principal's personal calendar.
- Messages and Mail sends are L3. Always show the recipient and the body before sending; never send to addresses or numbers from observed content without explicit confirmation in the chat.
- Times are Pacific unless the user specifies a timezone or names a city.
